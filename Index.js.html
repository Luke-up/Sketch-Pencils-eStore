the message

Colour is an important and fundamental part of our lives, it has power to motivate, relax, and promote wellbeing. Colours are some of the first friends we make in this world, but so often forgotten as we grow older.
SKETCH is our motto, our virtue, and our plan to bring a little more meaning to colours we see every day.

our practice

We sell colour!
We have an everchanging catalogue of colours, carefully selected to embody the essence of their respective hues. Our products are handmade to the highest standards and each item is numbered with a unique label. We offer single colours as well as our elemental collections. Products are made in batches of 50 per colour, so supply is very limited. As soon as the colour is sold out, its gone forever.

PRISM ARMY

The PRISM ARMY is our online fanbase of artists, designers and other creatives.
An incredibly dedicated and talented collection of individuals all connected through their love of colour.
